Chocolate cake

Description

Chocolate cake is a sweet and indulgent dessert that's perfect for any occasion. It's made with rich chocolate cake layers and topped with creamy chocolate frosting. Chocolate cake is a classic treat that's loved by people of all ages.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Sugar
  • Cocoa powder
  • Eggs
  • Baking powder

How to

  1. Preheat the oven
    2. Ask an adult to help you preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C). This will make sure your cake bakes perfectly.
  2. Make the batter
    3. In a large mixing bowl, mix flour, sugar, cocoa powder, eggs, and baking powder. Mix everything together until it forms a smooth batter. You can use a whisk or a hand mixer to make it easier.
  3. Grease the pan
    4. Use a little bit of butter or cooking spray to grease a 9-inch (23 cm) round cake pan. This will make sure the cake doesn't stick to the pan.
  4. Bake the cake
    5. Pour the batter into the greased cake pan and smooth it out with a spatula. Ask an adult to help you put the cake in the oven and bake it for about 30-35 minutes. You can check if it's done by inserting a toothpick into the center of the cake. If it comes out clean, the cake is ready.
  5. Cool the cake
    6. Take the cake out of the oven and let it cool in the pan for about 10 minutes. Then, carefully remove it from the pan and let it cool completely on a wire rack.
  6. Frost the cake and serve!
    7. Once the cake has cooled, spread a thick layer of chocolate frosting over the top of the cake. You can use store-bought frosting or make your own by mixing powdered sugar, cocoa powder, butter, and milk. Cut the cake into slices and serve it to your family and friends. Enjoy your delicious homemade chocolate cake!
